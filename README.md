# AirCanvas AI 🖌️

**Draw in the air. Create with your hands. Transform your ideas into reality.**

---

## 📖 About

AirCanvas AI is a cutting-edge computer vision project that transforms the way you create digital art. Using advanced hand gesture recognition powered by your webcam, you can draw, erase, and paint directly in the air with your fingers. No stylus, no touchscreen—just pure, intuitive, gesture-based creativity.

Perfect for:
- ✨ Interactive presentations
- 🎨 Digital art enthusiasts
- 🎓 Educational demonstrations
- 🎮 Gesture-based applications

---

## ✨ Features

- 🎯 **Real-Time Hand Tracking** - Accurately tracks your hand position and movements
- 🖍️ **Gesture-Based Drawing** - Draw smoothly and naturally with your finger
- 🗑️ **Smart Eraser** - Erase specific areas with precise control
- 🌈 **Dynamic Color Selection** - Choose from a vibrant color palette mid-draw
- ⚡ **Real-Time Processing** - Zero lag, instant visual feedback
- 💾 **Save Your Work** - Export your creations as images
- 📱 **Webcam Compatible** - Works with any standard USB or built-in camera
